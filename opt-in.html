SMS Consent & Messaging Program Disclosure

Sono IO — Messaging consent

This page explains exactly how consent is collected for SMS messages sent through Sono IO. This is separate and distinct from our general Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

The exact opt-in mechanism

Sono IO is a paid, single-message sending service. There is no account creation, subscription, or recurring messaging program. Each time a person uses Sono IO to send a message, they must actively check a standalone consent box before the message can be sent. This checkbox is unchecked by default and is not combined with payment, account creation, or any other action.

Who is consenting, and to what

Sono IO's messaging product works differently from a typical subscription SMS program. The sender — the person using our service and paying for it — is the party giving consent. They consent to: (1) Sono IO sending one message on their behalf to a number they provide, and (2) confirming they are authorized to contact that recipient. This consent is collected via the checkbox shown above, every single time, with no exceptions.

Sono IO does not collect, store, or solicit consent from message recipients, because recipients are not signing up for a recurring messaging program. Each message is a single, one-time, non-recurring text initiated and paid for by the sender.

Messaging program type

Informational

All Sono IO messages are informational, single-instance texts composed by the sender. No promotional or marketing content is ever sent by Sono IO itself.

Not promotional

Sono IO does not operate a promotional or marketing messaging program of any kind. We do not send bulk, automated, or recurring messages.

How to revoke consent / opt out

Any message recipient can reply STOP to any message received through Sono IO to immediately and permanently block that recipient's number from receiving any further messages via our platform. This is honored automatically and takes effect immediately. Recipients can also reply HELP for assistance, which returns our support contact information.

Message frequency

There is no recurring or scheduled messaging. Each message is a single, one-time send initiated by a human sender at the moment of purchase. A recipient cannot receive more than one message per sender transaction.

This page versus our other policies

This page describes only our SMS consent mechanism. It is intentionally separate from our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, which govern other aspects of using Sono IO. Agreeing to receive messages is never a requirement to use any other part of our service, and is collected exclusively through the standalone checkbox described above.
