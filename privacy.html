Sono IO — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you send a message through Sono IO, we collect: the message content (temporarily, to process and deliver your send), a SHA-256 hash of the message content (retained as proof of delivery), a one-way hash of the recipient's phone number (retained for abuse prevention and proof of delivery — the original number cannot be recovered from this hash), and a hashed version of your IP address (retained briefly for rate limiting and fraud prevention).

What we do not collect

Sono IO does not require account creation, does not collect your name, does not collect your real phone number unless you choose to provide one for call-related features, and does not store message content after a message has been delivered.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. Sono IO does not store your card number, billing address, or other payment details — these are handled entirely by Stripe in accordance with their own privacy policy.

How we use your information

Information collected is used solely to: deliver the message you requested, generate a proof-of-delivery record, prevent abuse and fraud, and comply with applicable carrier and legal requirements. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

Proof-of-delivery records (including the message hash, delivery status, and hashed recipient identifier) are retained for 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted. Message content itself is not retained after delivery.

Third-party services

Sono IO uses Twilio to deliver SMS messages and Stripe to process payments. Both providers maintain their own privacy policies governing data they process on our behalf.

Your rights

Message recipients can opt out of receiving any further messages at any time by replying STOP. For questions about data we may hold related to a specific transaction, contact us using the information below.
